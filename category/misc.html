One Post per Week

Published: Sat 16 January 2016
By Wei Liu

In Misc.

I'm now setting a new resolution: I plan to write one blog post per week.

I was sitting at the desktop watching random videos on the Internet this morning, then all of a sudden I felt hollow. I realized I would have wasted quite a lot of time just watching random videos on the Internet. Life is too short to do things like that.

As a matter of fact, I do have a lot of things that I want to write about. I accumulate topics quite fast because I read articles and books daily, so I don't think I will run out of ideas to rant about.

I have accumulated several drafts in my home directory but never got the motive to finish them. They sort of sit there rotting away until I eventually don't have the appetite to look at them anymore and delete them. This is quite bad.

The rule is simple: topic and length don't matter, just write one post a week. Rhetoric can be bad, grammar errors are allowed -- heck, I'm not aiming to become a professional writer or anything.

Let's see how it goes.
